Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your data is collected, used, and protected when you use the FinTrack mobile application (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and Google user ID to identify your account and provide synchronization.
  • Expense Data: All data you manually enter (expenses, categories, notes) is securely stored either on your device or in encrypted cloud storage linked to your account.
  • Device Information: We may collect technical information such as device model and operating system to improve performance and fix bugs.
  • AI Features: If you use AI-powered features, anonymized expense data may be temporarily processed by our AI provider to generate insights. No personal identifiers are shared.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized spending insights, analytics, and AI recommendations
  • To securely back up and sync your data if you're signed in
  • To improve app functionality and fix technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

All stored data is encrypted and access is restricted to your authenticated account. We use secure cloud infrastructure and follow industry best practices.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following services:

  • Google Sign-In – for secure authentication
  • Cloud storage providers – for secure backup (if signed in)
  • Analytics tools (optional) – for usage statistics and performance
  • AI service providers – for generating personalized insights

Each service has its own privacy policy.

5. No Advertising or Tracking

We do not use advertising networks. Your data is never sold or shared for marketing purposes.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your stored data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing (this may disable some features)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be published in the App and on our website.
