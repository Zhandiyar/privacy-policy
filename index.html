Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your data is collected, used, and protected when you use the FinTrack mobile application (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and Google user ID to identify your account and provide synchronization.
  • Expense Data: All data you manually enter (expenses, categories, notes) is securely stored either on your device or in encrypted cloud storage linked to your account.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic technical information such as device model and operating system to improve performance and fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide you with personalized spending insights and analytics
  • To securely back up and sync your data if you're signed in
  • To improve app functionality and fix technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously. All stored data is encrypted and access is restricted to your authenticated account. We use modern cloud infrastructure and follow best practices to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In – for secure authentication
  • Cloud storage providers – for secure data backup (if signed in)
  • Analytics tools (optional) – for usage statistics and performance monitoring

These services may collect limited information as described in their own privacy policies.

5. No Advertising or Tracking

We do not use any advertising networks. Your data is never shared or sold to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing (which may disable app features)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of major changes in the app or via email if available.
